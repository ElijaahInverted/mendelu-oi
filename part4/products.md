## Product 1: Indigo Wrap Jacket

**SKU:** TAN-JKT-001  
**Price:** 3 200 CZK  
**Category:** Jackets & Wraps

**Description:**  
Hand block-printed in Bagru, Rajasthan using natural indigo dye on 100% unbleached cotton. The print is a geometric lattice repeat — one of the oldest patterns in Bagru's woodblock repertoire, traced back to at least the 18th century. The jacket wraps and ties at the waist. No buttons, no zips. One size fits 36–42 (European sizing); the wrap allows 6–8 cm of adjustment in the chest.

The lining is terracotta-dyed cotton, same fabric, same workshop. Fabric weight: 180 g/m².

Wash at 30°C, inside out, away from direct sunlight when drying. Indigo fades gently over time — this is normal and expected with natural dyes.

**Available stock:** 12 units  
**Variants:** None — one print, one colourway, one size

## Product 5: Indigo Kantha Quilted Jacket

**SKU:** TAN-JKT-005  
**Price:** 4 800 CZK  
**Category:** Jackets & Wraps

**Description:**  
A heavier piece for autumn and early winter. Outer shell is block-printed cotton (indigo, geometric repeat); inner layer is recycled cotton batting; the two layers are hand-stitched together using the *kantha* running-stitch technique from West Bengal. Each jacket takes one artisan approximately 14 hours to complete.

The stitching pattern is concentric lines following the silhouette of the jacket. This is visible on the outside as a subtle texture — not decorative embroidery, just the structural stitching showing through.

Straight cut, collarless, hip-length. Five brass snap fasteners. Two patch pockets.

**Available stock:** 6 units (limited; no restock planned until AW2025)  
**Variants:** Size (S / M / L)
